Web Virtual Pet

Description

This is a project I completed during my first year at University. How it works: The pets stats slowly decrement over time, and the players job is to manage these stats by feeding, cleaning and putting the pet to sleep. If any of these stats hit 0, the pet will die. The happiness stat is calculated using the pet's food, sleep and cleanliness stats.
